Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT) from scratch

BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) is a groundbreaking language representation model developed by Google. Unlike previous models that read text either left-to-right or right-to-left, BERT reads entire sequences bidirectionally, allowing it to better understand the context of a word based on its surroundings. Trained on massive amounts of unlabeled text using tasks like masked language modeling and next sentence prediction, BERT has set new benchmarks across a wide range of NLP tasks including question answering, sentiment analysis, and named entity recognition. Its release marked a major shift in how language models are built and fine-tuned for downstream tasks.
